Homing In Purchaser Refund Policy

a. Nonrefundable.

All membership purchases are non-refundable.

b. Term.

Premium and Elite Memberships run month-to month. They will auto renew each month unless cancelled by the purchaser. To cancel a subscription email support@homingin.co with subject CANCEL MY MEMBERSHIP and full member details including name, email, phone, company name. Refunds will not be pro-rated.

c. No Early Termination Fees.

Membership will continue to run until month's service ends.

d. Termination.

Homing In LLC reserves the right to terminate any membership at any time.
